The game takes place on a fantasy-themed island called “Opal Island”, where you build and improve your town, collect treasures and assemble teams of unique heroes to fight powerful creatures.

Form your team, explore mysterious places and fight in epic battles to collect resources, NFTs and GNG tokens, the Gems & Goblins cryptocurrency.

Are you ready to assemble the most fearsome team of heroes the Opal Islands has ever seen? Will you take on the awful goblins in search of precious pink gems? Do you have what it takes to become part of the Legendary Army?

Gems & Goblins is a play and earn game that takes you on a journey of mysterious exploration, heroic battles, and epic rewards. Join others in epic PvP battles, rise through the ranks and earn GNG tokens to make in-game purchases to strengthen your heroes, and enhance your town!

You can trade your rewards with other players in the Gemwelry Marketplace, or make passive income by staking or farming your GNG tokens through the supported exchanges.

Gems & Goblins is based on the latest blockchain and cryptocurrency innovations that enable players to earn real money while playing their favorite games. Thanks to its blockchain roots, it’s safe, uncheatable, and fair to everyone.

By playing Gems & Goblins and using the GNG currency, you enable us, the developers of the game, to implement new features and ideas. This will draw more players to the game, resulting in a better gaming experience for the players, while increasing the value of investor’s capital.

The native currency of the game is the Gems & Goblins (GNG) token, which can be traded, staked, and farmed. Passive income mechanisms guarantee rewards to users and long-term holders simply by holding their tokens.

GNG tokens can also be traded for resources, extra weapons, skills, and other improvements for your heroes, and see the value of your tokens increase through the built-in deflation mechanisms.

Gems & Goblins operates on top of Callisto Network, a layer 1 Ethereum Compatible smart contract solution based on proof-of-work blockchain. Powered by its native cryptocurrency, CLO, Callisto Network is fast, safe, and cheap, with a cost below 0.0001 USD per transaction, combined with a 51% attacks protection.

Callisto Network is the blockchain as seen by Security Experts. It has made numerous contributions to improve $ETH, $ETC, $EOS and audited over 360 smart contracts, establishing the team as the leader in the crypto-security industry.
